Flooring in Lynchburg

Flooring for Lynchburg Homes

Lynchburg's diverse housing stock means flooring needs vary widely. The historic homes in Rivermont and Boonsboro often have original hardwood that needs refinishing or repair, while newer homes near Timberlake and Wyndhurst may be ready for an upgrade from builder-grade carpet or laminate.

We install and repair all flooring types — hardwood, engineered wood, ceramic and porcelain tile, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), and laminate. We also handle subfloor repair, which is especially important in Lynchburg's older homes where years of settling and moisture can cause uneven surfaces.
